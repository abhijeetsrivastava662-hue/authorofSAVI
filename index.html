The Story

Some stories are imagined.
This one was witnessed.

SAVI is not just a novel. It is a preservation of moments before time could erase them — the story of Agastya and Savi, rooted in hyper-realistic emotional truth, intimacy, and the fragile beauty of human connection.

Through poetic prose and cinematic atmosphere, this book transforms the ordinary into the unforgettable:

  • A late-night text becomes a lifetime memory.
  • A silver ring becomes a silent promise.
  • A crowded street becomes the setting of heartbreak.
  • A single glance becomes an unspoken confession of love.

The Unfinished Dream
"He lived around its heartbeat."

Agastya always carried a quiet dream — to someday write a book about love with honesty, depth, and soul. A story that would make people feel seen, remaining with them long after the final page.

♥

But sometimes life leaves certain stories unfinished.

To honor that unfulfilled dream, his childhood best friend, Sumit Pandey, stepped in. Not as a distant storyteller, but as a witness who stood close enough to experience the late-night conversations, the healing laughter, and the unbearable silences that changed everything.

♥

SAVI is that unwritten dream, finally given a voice.

The Experience

Cinematic Realism

Sentences that feel like whispers. Prose that captures the exact, raw ache of longing — the unnamed weight that lives between two people.

About the Author
Age 21 · Debut Novel

Sumit Pandey

Sumit Pandey emerges not only as a writer, but as an observer of raw human emotion — someone who notices the silences between words, the pain hidden behind smiles, and the fragile beauty of connection.

Quietly sophisticated and deeply thoughtful, Sumit possesses a rare sensitivity toward life. He remembers emotions the way others remember events. Where most people hear conversations, he uncovers meaning. Where others move on from moments, he preserves them forever through words.

Through SAVI, he ensures that a beautiful, heartbreaking legacy echoes forever.
